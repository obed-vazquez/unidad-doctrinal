Why do we believe God exists?

  1. Reasons

  1. Order and the design in the cosmos point to a designer
  2. Everything that has a beginning has a cause (The Universe has a beginning)
  3. Entropic principle. It is not logical to think that everything has happened by chance.
  4. The amount of information and design in the smallest systems of the universe is so densely packed that it demands an intelligent mind as a designer.
  5. It’s more reasonable to believe that Life comes from life than from non-life.
  6. God's purpose is the only thing that really fulfills the desire and innate drive of humans for meaning in life, and it is best explained by the fact that we were created for a purpose
  1. Humans have a drive to believe in life after death, which will demand a God that provides it.
  1. Because the human conscience that ties us (me) to moral absolutes demands a moral law-giver
  2. Because we (I) experience love.
  3. Because I experience free will, that demonstrates that we are not programmed but created with this free will
  1. The characteristics of this intelligent being that will fulfill this set of requirements is tied to the Biblical God through Jesus.
