Belief system essence defining inquiries

  1. Definition

  1. These inquiries are generated to define the core values and importance to humanity of a religion or belief. Asking these questions should provide meaningful information about a belief and give us the relative rate of importance of a religion or belief system.

  1. Belief system essence defining inquiry set

  1. What is the meaning and purpose of life?
  2. What happens after death, and what is the nature of eternity or the afterlife?
  3. How do beliefs address or provide solutions to the challenges or "Human problem" of human existence, such as suffering, moral dilemmas, or the quest for happiness?
  4. How do beliefs shape our values, ethics, and worldview?
  5. What is the relationship between beliefs, identity, and personal fulfillment?
  6. What role does the belief system play in guiding ethical decision-making and moral values?
  7. How does the belief system address the concept of truth and knowledge?
