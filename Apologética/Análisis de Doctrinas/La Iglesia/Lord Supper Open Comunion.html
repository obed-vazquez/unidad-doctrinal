There are 3 type of teaching in the administration of the Lord's Supper referring to its participants.

  1. Open Communion.  The church allows that any person take the Holy Supper.
  2. Denominational or Close Communion. Only people who are members of a church with the same denomination can join to take the Holy Supper.
  3. Closed Communion. Only the local church can take the Lord's Supper.

In here the 3 of them are being analyzed but the analysis will focus on the of the doctrine of open communion.
